Hi, I'm Marg

I originally started Marg's Beauty Spot Beauty Salon in 1994 and enjoyed the twelve years of great business and awesome clients in the Carlton Courts. After a break of four years, I now have a gorgeous new spot in Heathcote Valley. It's a home business which is perfect for my lifestyle.

I look forward to welcoming both old and new clients for all your Thalgo facials, Brazilian and bikini waxing, and other beauty therapy treatments - take a look through my website and give me a call, text or email. I would love to hear from you!

Thalgo Facials

Rediscover the treasures of marine beauty care.

Full of indescribable beauty, the sea has been our passion for more than forty years. Thalgo has gathered from the oceans and seas the very best actives in order to develop authentic and efficient cosmetics. The sea has inspired us with the treatment approach and experiences combining spa wellbeing and beauty results. Today, Thalgo's excellencies are to be discovered in the most beautiful and prestigious salons, spas and thalassotherapy centres around the world. We invite you to discover Thalgo and explore new sensations ...
